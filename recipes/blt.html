BLT Recipe

Description

The BLT sandwich, short for bacon, lettuce, and tomato, is a timeless culinary classic cherished for its straightforward yet delicious flavors. It begins with two slices of toasted bread, providing a crispy foundation. The highlight is the bacon, cooked to a perfect crisp yet maintaining a bit of chewiness, offering a smoky, savory flavor. Crunchy lettuce and ripe, sweet-tangy tomatoes complement the bacon, while a layer of creamy mayonnaise unifies the ingredients. This sandwich is often enjoyed for lunch or a casual dinner, embodying a harmonious blend of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white or whole grain)
  • 3-4 slices of bacon
  • 2-3 leaves of lettuce (iceberg or romaine)
  • 2 slices of ripe tomato
  • Mayonnaise, to taste
  • Optional: Avocado slices, cheese, or a fried egg for variation
